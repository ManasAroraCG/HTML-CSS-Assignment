About CG Infinity

We build useful tech and keep things practical. This is a quick intro to our team.

Our Company

CG Infinity started in 1998 with one goal: build software that solves real problems. Today we work with 500+ clients in different parts of the world.

Our Mission

We help businesses grow using practical technology, not just fancy demos. If it is not useful in real work, we do not ship it.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a trusted technology partner for teams that want long-term, stable products.

Founder

Arjun Kumar founded CG Infinity with 15+ years of industry experience. His approach has always been user-first product building.

Leadership

Our leadership team has worked in startups and enterprises across India, the US, and Europe, and they bring those learnings into daily execution here.
